About It

The Philippines is an archepelagic country in Southeast Asia. Right here you can find beautiful tourist spots and places to visit and enjoy. So here are the 3 destinations that are recommended for you.


Boracay

Boracay is one of the world's top destinations for relaxation.

This beach has a beautiful view and white sand.

Bohol

Bohol is also a wonderful destination to go in, aside from Boracay.

Its famous for having the chocolate hills, and tarsier.

Palawan

Palawan is even a good place to have some relaxation just like Bohol and Boracay.

Just like Boracay, it's also famous for having a beautiful beach and white sand, but there is so much more about the beach, there are coral reefs, awesome limestone rock formations and amazingly turquoise colored waters.
